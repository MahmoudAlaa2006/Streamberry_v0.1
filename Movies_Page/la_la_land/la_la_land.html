La La Land

La La Land is a 2016 American musical romantic drama film written and directed by Damien Chazelle. It stars Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone as a struggling jazz pianist and an aspiring actress, and two men who fall in love while pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles. The film stars John Legend, Rosemarie DeWitt, Finn Witt, and J.K. Simmons.
